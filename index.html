Traveler

Web Development

Programming of a customized trip planning website. Followed important feature of responsive web design. Developed the website by the HTML/CSS3/jQuery.
Traveler is a customized trip planning website that people can locate their destinations, discover interesting places, and schedule their trip in one website.
The reason that I design this website is I love traveling. I used to plan my trip through many different ways, which I think that’s very inconvenient. Thus, I decided to create a website that can help traveler to plan their trips much easier.
